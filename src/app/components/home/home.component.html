Frequently Asked Questions

Which accessories are included?

Every KOYO includes a USB‑C cable, SIM tool, and protective case. Chargers are available separately to reduce e‑waste.

How long will my phone receive updates?

KOYO guarantees 3 years of Android OS upgrades and 4 years of monthly security updates from the launch of your model.

Is KOYO compatible with my carrier?

KOYO models support major global 5G and 4G LTE bands. Check the model page for band details and carrier certification in your country.

What is the warranty?

A 2‑year limited warranty covers manufacturing defects. Extended coverage and accidental damage protection are available at checkout.

Do you support eSIM?

Yes. All current models support eSIM and one physical nano‑SIM for flexible dual‑line setups.

What is your return policy?

You can return your phone within 15 days in original condition for a full refund. We provide a prepaid return label in supported regions.
